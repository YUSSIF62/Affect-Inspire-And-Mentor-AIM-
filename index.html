Affect Inspire and Mentor (AIM)

Affect Inspire and Mentor (AIM) is a non-government organization dedicated to empowering orphaned girls in northern Ghana who haven't had the opportunity to attend school. We believe every girl deserves a chance to reach her full potential.

What do we offer

Affect Inspire and Mentor (AIM)inclusion program is to inspire orphaned girls who are school dropouts or did not get the opportunity to go school. We underwent research in rural communities in the northern part of Ghana to find out if this situation still exists. Yes ! There are more of such cases in deprieved communities. We are empowering four of these girls in apprenticeship!

  • All girls stay in a rented apartment

    We provide comfortable housing in a rented apartment, ensuring a safe and secure environment.

  • Provision of Basic Needs

    We provide them all their basic needs such as food , accommodation, electricity, water and many more

  • Provision of Equipments

    We equip them with the tools they need to succeed, providing equipment like sewing machines for their chosen vocational training.

  • Mentorship

    Our dedicated mentors guide them through personal and reproductive health education, along with navigating daily life challenges.

Our Vision

Our long-term goal is to create a permanent home and skills training center. This center will provide a nurturing environment where girls can learn diverse skills aligned with their interests, empowering them to build brighter futures.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of orphaned girls in northern Ghana. You can be a part of their journey by

  • Volunteering

  • Spreading Awareness
